# Google Cloud Generative AI Leader

_Last Updated: November 30, 2025_

This path targets business leaders, consultants, and decision‑makers who need to:

- Understand core Generative AI (GenAI) concepts and terminology
- Map Google Cloud GenAI capabilities (Vertex AI, Gemini, Model Garden) to business outcomes
- Evaluate ROI, risks, and ethical implications of GenAI
- Communicate GenAI strategy to both technical and non‑technical stakeholders

Start with `exam-guide.md` for the official exam overview, then follow:

- `genai-fundamentals.md` – foundations and terminology
- `vertex-ai-overview.md` – GenAI capabilities on Google Cloud
- `business-use-cases.md` – scenario‑driven use cases (including healthcare)
- `responsible-ai.md` – governance, ethics, and risk management
- `study-materials.md` – Skills Boost paths and recommended reading
- `practice-questions.md` – exam‑style practice items

For a time‑boxed plan, see the Generative AI Leader section in `../study-plan.md`.
