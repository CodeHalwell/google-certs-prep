# Exam Guide – Google Cloud Generative AI Leader

_Last Updated: November 30, 2025_

- **Exam format:** 90 minutes, multiple‑choice and multiple‑select
- **Cost:** Approximately **£74** (about **$99 USD**) – check current pricing in your region
- **Delivery:** Online or test centre via Kryterion / Webassessor (subject to change)
- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Recommended background:**
  - Familiarity with cloud and AI concepts
  - Experience making or advising on technology decisions
  - Exposure to GenAI use cases within an organisation

## Skills Assessed

- [ ] GenAI fundamentals and terminology
- [ ] Google Cloud GenAI offerings (Vertex AI, Gemini, Model Garden, PaLM API heritage)
- [ ] Model selection and optimisation for business needs
- [ ] Business use cases, value propositions, and ROI
- [ ] Responsible AI principles and risk management
- [ ] GenAI strategy, road‑mapping, and change management

Use this guide together with `study-materials.md` and `business-use-cases.md`.
