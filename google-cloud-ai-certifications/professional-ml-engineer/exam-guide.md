# Exam Guide – Google Cloud Professional Machine Learning Engineer

_Last Updated: November 30, 2025_

- **Exam format:** 2 hours, approximately 50–60 multiple‑choice/multiple‑select questions
- **Cost:** Approximately **£150** (about **$200 USD**) plus local taxes
- **Delivery:** Online or test centre via Kryterion / Webassessor (verify when booking)
- **Recommended experience:**
  - 3+ years of industry experience in ML/DS/engineering
  - 1+ year designing, building, and deploying ML solutions on Google Cloud
- **Certification validity:** 2 years
- **Passing score:** Typically around 70% (Google does not publish an exact value; treat 70–75% as a safe target)

## Exam Domains and Weights

- [ ] **Frame ML problems** – **10–15%**
- [ ] **Architect ML solutions** – **20–25%**
- [ ] **Design data preparation and processing systems** – **20–25%**
- [ ] **Develop ML models** – **25–30%**
- [ ] **Automate and orchestrate ML pipelines** – **15–20%**
- [ ] **Monitor, optimise, and maintain ML solutions** – **10–15%**

See `exam-domains.md` for checklists, and `study-schedule.md` for a week‑by‑week plan.
